Usizo Humane Donations

This is a platform for people and businesses to donate to patients in hospitals who need medical care but cannot afford the fees. It’s by passes the need to for paying NGOs since Donors will immediately be able to see their money make an impact on a person’s life. This idea actually came to us from Hackathon that we attended. We won the first place price prize and we were able to fund the development of the platform. We are still in the late stages of development on this app and we expect to launch early 2019. You can read the full article of the hackathon here.
